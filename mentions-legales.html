Data and sources

13FLOW aggregates and formats public SEC EDGAR filings, including 13F-HR institutional holdings and Form 4 ownership reports. SEC filings are U.S. public-domain government records. CUSIP-to-ticker mapping may use OpenFIGI metadata.

Data are provided “as is” for information and research. Filing delays, source amendments, issuer mistakes and mapping gaps can occur; source accessions and public verification endpoints are exposed for auditability.

No investment advice

13FLOW is a screening and research tool. Scores, rankings, Confluence views and API responses are not personalised recommendations, investment advice, solicitation, execution guidance, probability estimates or performance promises. You remain responsible for your own diligence and regulatory framework.

Privacy and cookies

The public site is read-only: it does not require a user account, does not request personal data and does not set advertising or analytics cookies. Fonts are self-hosted from the same domain; no Google Fonts request is required for public rendering.

Technical server logs such as IP address, timestamp, user-agent and requested resource may be retained temporarily for security, abuse prevention and service reliability.

LIVE, DEMO and Pro surfaces

The main public domain runs in a verifiable LIVE mode exposed through /api/live-status and /api/version. Demo mode must be explicit and must not be confused with production data.

The public site has no checkout, online payment or account creation. Pro access, when contracted, uses API keys, quotas, audit logs and separate commercial terms covering service levels, support and reversibility.

Intellectual property and liability

The interface, editorial text, name and logo are protected. Underlying SEC records remain reusable from their public source. 13FLOW works to keep the site available and accurate, but cannot guarantee uninterrupted access, complete data, error-free filings or suitability for any investment process.
